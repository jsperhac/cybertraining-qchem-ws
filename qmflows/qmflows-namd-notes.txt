Notes about qmflows-namd

conflicts
=========
	qmflows-namd wants 
		libint 2.4.2 (psi4 needs libint 1.3.2)
		highfive, which needs hdf5 >= 1.10.4 (psi4 needs hdf5 between 1.10.2 and 1.10.3)
		
	libint (1.3.2) for psi4 conflicts with libint (2.4.2) for qmflows-namd
